Account and Data Deletion

Public instructions for Google Play compliance

If you wish to delete your account and associated personal data from Smar8 Solutions services, please follow the steps below. You do not need to be logged in to make this request.

How to Request Deletion

Email us at contact@smar8.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your registered email address or phone number
  • Your full name (as used in the app)
  • Optional: Your user ID (if available)
  • A statement confirming you want permanent deletion of your account and data

What Will Be Deleted

Your account profile and personal usage data associated with the account will be deleted. We may retain records that are legally required (e.g., invoices, transaction confirmations) for the minimum period mandated by law.

Verification

For security, we may request verification via the registered email or phone number before proceeding with deletion.

Timeline

We acknowledge requests within 2 business days and complete deletion within 30 days, unless legal obligations require a different period.
